The Marble Clinic Restoration

Based in Los Angeles, CA, the Marble Clinic Restoration is a full-service restoration and maintenance company servicing different kinds of stones such as Travertine, Limestone, Terrazzo, Slate, Ceramic, Pavers, Brick, Concrete, Marble, Granite, and more. We specialize in honing, polishing, refinishing, resurfacing, and repairing natural stones such as marble. Marbles or stones that have become dull, scratched, etched, cracked, chipped, or contain dirt, grime, grout, hard water stains, or are old stones do not have to be replaced. We promise to restore your marble or stone to its original state even better sometimes.

No matter the problem with your natural stone floors, tiles, countertops, or solid surfaces, we are capable of restoring marble or stone to their best form!

Since we are specialists in stone maintenance and care, our staff is knowledgeable in this field with lots of professional & personal experience to guarantee your stones get the best restoration services.

About Us

The companys founders made the company with a strong passion for the care & maintenance of different stones.
